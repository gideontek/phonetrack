Location sharing that respects your privacy and your data plan. PhoneTrack SMS responds to text message requests with your GPS coordinates — no internet required, no account needed, no data used.

A trusted contact texts your keyword and the phone texts back a location and an OpenStreetMap link. Need regular check-ins? Subscribe for periodic updates that include how far and which direction you've moved. The sender doesn't even need the app installed.

New requests are held in a PENDING state until you decide: APPROVED numbers always get a reply, BLOCKED numbers are quietly ignored.

Features:
* One-shot location request: reply with GPS fix + OpenStreetMap link
* Subscriptions: periodic updates with heading and distance delta (e.g. ⇐314m)
* Approval gate: PENDING → APPROVED / BLOCKED
* Zero internet permission — SMS only, always
* No account, no cloud, no tracking
* Android 8.0 (API 26) and later
